# Andrew Dyer - Vice President

Andrew is a Vice President at Dark Alpha Capital, an investment firm focused on acquiring private companies across the lower middle market having $1,000,000 plus of EBITDA. Andrew’s responsibilities include sourcing, evaluating, and enabling the growth of Dark Alpha's investments.

Prior to joining Dark Alpha, Andrew worked as the President and CEO of Institutional Acquisition Partners. Before joining Institutional Acquisition Partners, Andrew worked as an investment associate with a private family office in Dallas, as a Founder and CEO of BonADfide Technologies, as an associate at Stanford Management Company, and as an investment associate at Arcady Asset Management.

Andrew received a BS in Finance from Texas Christian University.
