# About Peter Kim

With over a decade of investment banking, law, and private equity experience, Peter brings about robust strategic and analytical expertise to drive deal success. He has been actively involved in over 50 transactions totalling over $250 million in the energy and healthcare sectors.

Peter possesses extensive advisory experience in finance, revenue optimization, data management, and analytics. With a quantitative foundation, he regularly employs modeling, scenario planning, simulation, and data mining techniques.

Educationally, Peter holds a BBA from UT Austin and a JD from the University of Houston. He is a licensed member of the State Bar of Texas, a Chartered Alternative Investment Analyst, and maintains several finance and real estate licenses.

With his diverse transactional background and licensure, Peter is skilled at diligently executing all aspects of complex deals. His value-add operations and advisory capabilities in areas like modeling, analytics, and data optimization accelerate growth.
