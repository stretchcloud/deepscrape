# David Kopplin - Managing Director

David is a Managing Director at Dark Alpha Capital, an investment firm focused on acquiring private companies across the lower middle market having $1,000,000 plus of EBITDA. David’s responsibilities include sourcing, evaluating, and enabling the growth of Dark Alpha's investments.

Prior to joining Dark Alpha, David worked as a Managing Director, Corporate Finance and Strategy, at Groce, Rose, & Moore LLC. Before joining Groce, Rose, & Moore LLC, David worked as an Assistant Vice President, Debt Management, at the University of North Carolina System, as a Director, Investment Banking and Consulting, at Taylor Advisory, and as a Director, M&A and Business Analysis, at H&E Equipment Services, Inc.

David received a B.A.Sc. in Forest Resources Management from the University of Idaho.
