## Driving Growth: Meet Our Operating Partners

Our Operating Partners are seasoned professionals who play a hands-on role in driving value and growth across our portfolio. Drawing on deep operational expertise and proven leadership experience, they work side-by-side with management teams to identify opportunities, implement strategic initiatives, and enhance overall performance. By bringing best practices, resources, and mentorship to each engagement, our Operating Partners ensure every business we support remains agile, efficient, and positioned for long-term success. Through ongoing collaboration and a commitment to operational excellence, they help translate vision into tangible results, fueling sustainable growth at every stage of the journey.
